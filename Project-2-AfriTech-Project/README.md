## 2. Business Problem Statement
Afritech has faced challenges affecting its brand reputation: negative social media buzz, rising customer complaints, product recalls, and competitive pressure. This has led to:

- Declining customer trust  
- Reduced sales and market share  
- Increased dissatisfaction from slow support  
- Competitors exploiting reputation gaps  

---

## 3. Project Rationale
Social media strongly influences public perception. Monitoring it allows Afritech to:

- Detect PR risks early  
- Engage with customers promptly  
- Manage crises effectively  
- Learn from trends for marketing and product improvements  


---

## 4. Aims & Objectives
- Protect market position by maintaining positive brand reputation  
- Retain customers through timely issue resolution  
- Mitigate crises using early warning systems  
- Enable data-driven decision-making from social media insights  
- Improve marketing campaigns through customer sentiment analysis

## 11. Recommendations
